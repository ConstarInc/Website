Steam Turbine Control

SINOPEC Yizheng DAE Co-Generation Steam Turbine Control Project

Constar delivers turnkey projects and comprehensive services of steam turbine control system. One the one hand, Constar works closely and keeps long-standing relationship with various turbine manufactures, including Harbin Turbine Works, Qingdao Jieneng Turbine Works, Siemens Industrial Turbine, etc., to develop the performance-driven control solutions, making OEM's steam turbine more competitive. On the other hand, Constar has successfully directly installed dozens of steam turbine control system in the petrochemical, steel, power generation fields.

Focusing on energy control solution for twenty years, Constar has developed strong engineering expertise of steam turbine control system, long-standing relationship with customers, and a professional engineering team. Also, with the rich hands-on engineering experience, the understanding of unique requirement in China market, and efficient cross culture communication, Constar successfully integrates world's leading technology with China local engineering strength, and has successfully installed dozens of turbine control and protection projects, ranging from small industrial turbine to 1000MW large steam turbine for nuclear power station, and our customers include SINOPEC, CNPC, CNOOC, An Steel, Bao Steel, etc.

SINOPEC Jinling Petro Unit1 DEH Project
SINOPEC Jinling Petro Unit1 DEH Project

SINOPEC Jinling Petro Unit1 is a 60MW co-generation genset, which was installed in 1990's by Shanghai Steam Turbine Works. The turbine was originally installed with hydraulic governors regulating turbine power and two extraction steam pressures. Over decades of operation, the hydraulic control system sufferred leaking, friction and deteriorated accuracy.

Constar upgraded the turbine hydraulic governor with Woodward Micronet TMR control system in year 2002. The upgraded DEH is TMR(Triple Module Redundancy) in hardware and powered with Ratio/Limiter algorithm. The upgraded control system not only improved reliability and accuracy, but also made operation more friendly. Since installation, the genset has been working stably and safely for almost twenty years, demonstrating the strong engineering capability of Constar.

  • Turbine Type: Co-Generation Genset, Double Automatic Extration Turbine
  • Rated Power: 60MW
  • Turbine Manufacture: Shanghai Steam Turbine Works
  • Control System: Micronet TMR
  • Application Type: DEH Upgrade
  • Date: Year 2002
