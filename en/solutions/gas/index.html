Gas Turbine Control

Provide Technical Support to Liyuan Gas Power Plant

To reduce emissions and diversify energy portfolio, China is booting manufacture and installations of gas turbine, making it an increasingly exciting market for gas turbine technology.

Constar has been working closely with both gas turbine manufactures and end users to develop more cost-effective gas turbine control solutions for more than ten years. Meanwhile, Constar provides comprehensive services to customers, including Harbin Turbine Works, CSIC Longjiang GH Turbine, CNPC, CNOOC, Huadian Group, etc.
